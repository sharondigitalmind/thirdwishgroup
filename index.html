Social Media Marketing

Social media marketing is building your brand on the social media channels. You make contact with your target group with social media marketing. This stimulates visitors, for example, to go to your website and increases your (online) sales. The content you post must match your target audience. Make sure you listen to your target group (so not only send but also receive) and regularly analyze the results of your strategy. The goal is to make people (potential) customers fans of your brand!

The main social media channels (at the moment) are Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, LinkedIn, Pinterest, YouTube and Snapchat. However, some channels may not suit your target group at all, and therefore cannot simply be part of your social media strategy.

How we can help with your social Media brand

We make examples of the content we have in mind for your social media channels in advance. During the process of your social media marketing, we keep in close contact with each other. You can always ask questions, and we will regularly send you samples of the posts we have made for you.  

Every brand is different, the target group is different, the platforms on which the target group can be reached is different and the forms of address are different. We are happy to talk to you about your company and your goals. Based on that, we make a plan; we examine your target group and the social media platforms where marketing is useful for your company. In this plan we also describe the concept for your expressions.

We have various packages for social media marketing, where we can always take your budget into account. Feel free to contact us for an informal conversation!

GOOGLE OPTIMIZATION (SEO) OF YOUR WEBSITE

What Can Sharon do for you!

SEO is short for "search engine optimization", or optimizing your website for search engines such as Google and Bing. This means that your WordPress website will appear higher in Google search results, using the correct search terms. The important thing is that you can be found not only on your company name, but also on your services. A well-optimized website has to do with various aspects, including text, links, image optimization and a number of technical improvements to the website, which will allow Google to place the website higher in the search results. We can never promise that your website will be on top in Google, this depends on too many factors. Who promised you this is debatable as far as we are concerned; no one has 100% influence on the number 1 position in Google. I wish it was true!
In addition to SEO, you can also use SEA (Search Engine Advertising), (we can help you with that too). SEA, as the name implies, stands for advertising on Google. This works well, but it is important that your SEO is well organized so that you are not dependent on the advertisements. SEA is a good addition to your online marketing strategy.

WHAT CAN SHARON DO FOR YOUR WEBSITE?

Website content - Are the texts Google friendly? Are the correct search terms reflected in your texts in a good way?
User-friendliness - Is your WordPress website clear to visitors? Is the structure of your website correct?
Technology - By streamlining the technology, the site is easier to read for Google
Speed - The loading time of your WordPress website is an important criterion for Google
Competition - How is the competition doing in Google? Which search terms do they use?
