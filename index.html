What we are all about

Universal Accounting & Tax services can easily be tailored made to suit our client’s needs. Our pricing is very competitive as we consider factors such as the clients size, turnover, budget, affordability, and profitability when determining our fees. We believe in investing in our clients by offering affordable rates at our initial engagements and later adjust in line with their liquidity and profitability requirements. We specialise in three key areas namely Accounting, Tax and Compliance. at the same time maintaining integrity and confidently of our client data. Our team is composed of experienced and highly qualified individuals to offer our customers good services.
